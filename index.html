About Us

Established in 2022, PT. Maritim Armada Raya specializes in domestic sea freight, focusing on bulk coal transport across Indonesia with a reliable fleet and strong partnerships.

Vision

To be a leading regional sea transportation company, providing safe and reliable services.

Mission

  • Establish strong, beneficial partnerships with stakeholders.
  • Enhance human resource competencies.
  • Add value to mining logistics transport.

Our Services

We specialize in carrying bulk commodities using various sizes of tugs and barges for both transshipment and domestic shipment.

Domestic Shipment

Our domestic shipping services connect multiple loading ports in Kalimantan and Sumatera with discharging ports across Sulawesi, Sumatera, and Java Island.

Transshipment

We provide transshipment services at various locations, including Taboneso, Satui, and Tanjung Pemancingan.
