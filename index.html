About Us

PT Maritim Armada Raya, established in March 2022, specializes in domestic sea transportation within Indonesia, the world's largest archipelago. Focused on bulk coal transport, we play a key role in the growing coal energy sector. Supported by reputable shipping partners, our tugs and barges ensure safe and efficient delivery, driving innovation and excellence in maritime logistics.

Our Vision

To become a first-class sea transportation company in the region, known for excellent service and secure deliveries.

Our Mission

  • Build mutually beneficial partnerships with stakeholders.
  • Develop human resource competencies.
  • Create added value for mining logistics transportation.
