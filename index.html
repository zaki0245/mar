ABOUT US

Founded in March 2022, PT. Maritim Armada Raya specializes in domestic sea freight, focusing on bulk coal transportation—a key player in Indonesia’s logistics for the energy sector. Reliable sea transportation is vital in supporting the rapidly growing coal industry and broader logistics demands in Indonesia.

Vision

To become a first-class sea transportation company in the region, known for excellent service and secure deliveries.

Mission

  • Build mutually beneficial partnerships with all stakeholders.
  • Develop human resource competencies.
  • Create added value for mining logistics transportation.

OUR SERVICES

We specialize in carrying bulk commodities using various sizes of tugs and barges for both transshipment and domestic shipment.

Domestic Shipment

Our domestic shipping services connect multiple loading ports in Kalimantan and Sumatera with discharging ports across Sulawesi, Sumatera, and Java Island.

Transshipment

We provide transshipment services at various locations, including Taboneso, Satui, and Tanjung Pemancingan.
