About Us

Founded in March 2022, PT. Maritim Armada Raya specializes in domestic sea freight, focusing on bulk coal transportation—a key player in Indonesia’s logistics for the energy sector. Reliable sea transportation is vital in supporting the rapidly growing coal industry and broader logistics demands in Indonesia.

Vision

To become a first-class sea transportation company in the region, known for excellent service and secure deliveries.

Mission

  • Build mutually beneficial partnerships with all stakeholders.
  • Develop human resource competencies.
  • Create added value for mining logistics transportation.

Our Services

PT. Maritim Armada Raya specializes in transporting bulk commodities using various sizes of tugs and barges for both transshipment and domestic shipment.

Domestic Shipment

We serve domestic shipments from multiple loading ports in Kalimantan and Sumatera to discharge ports across Sulawesi, Sumatera, and Java Island. Our services ensure efficient and timely delivery, meeting the logistical needs throughout Indonesia's archipelago.

Transshipment

We also provide transshipment services at major locations such as Taboneso, Satui, and Tanjung Pemancingan, among others. Our transshipment services facilitate coal distribution from loading ports to larger vessels, improving logistics efficiency for our clients.
